History Of Dabong

Dabong, also known as Kuala Krai Selatan, is a small town in Kuala Krai District, Kelantan, Malaysia. It contains a train station. On the Galas River, the famous Gua Ikan (Fish Cave) is located nearby. One of the four state seats in the Kuala Krai constituency is Dabong. Dabong is a very strategic town that could be chosen as the new capital of Kelantan due to its central location in the state and its accessibility from all areas of Kelantan. Years of failed state development plans have left Kelantan with an unbalanced population and a pattern of dispersed major cities.
